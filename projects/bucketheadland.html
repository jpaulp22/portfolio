Bucketheadland

Remodeling Buckethead's website with a modern, simplistic approach

Buckethead is a world-famous guitar player and is regarded as one of the best shredders of all time. A peculiar figure, Buckethead stays true to his persona, rarely verbally communicating and being very reserved with their personal life. My goal was to redesign Buckethead's webpage. As of the finalization of this short project, his website was just a landing page with images in a column, some leading to other pages while others just for display purposes. This site uses HTML, CSS, and the model viewer API.

I began by creating low-fidelity wireframes to get a basic idea of how I was going to structure a sampling of the content. If you know Buckethead, you know there's over 100+ albums. My goal here was to produce just a small snippit of what could be.

This website is mobile friendly and was my one of my first creative projects. It was challenging getting the model to work as well as the text animation when you load the page on desktop. You can find the website here and a much more in depth look at the final creation on Behance!
