Web Designer. Musician. Vlogger.

Hi! My name is Jean Paul Perez-Saldarriaga. I am a web designer from NYC currently completing my studies in Emerging Media at Hunter College. Originally a computer science major, I took up front-end development when I came to the realization I wanted to be involved more with the visual aspects of the web. I've always been close with the arts, primarily through music. I enjoy creating. Creating new ideas be it through music or through visuals. When I'm not spending time helping clients make their next big step in their business journey, I'm either learning about new frameworks, doing print design, writing music, or vlogging.

I've been playing guitar for 8 years and I'm an occasional busker playing some jazz with friends. I absolutely love traveling and exploring new places, so much that I have a Youtube channel with my girlfriend dedicated to our travels. I'm open to all genres of music but have a sweet spot for 80s music and alternative music (occasionally making public playlists on Spotify). Want to work together or send me a message?
